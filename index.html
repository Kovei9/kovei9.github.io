About Me

I'm Kenzie — a 2D/3D Generalist specializing in animation. I work best wearing multiple hats: 2D, 3D, rigging, VFX, shader graphs, and implementation in Unity and Unreal.

During university I worked for two studios (GIMM Works), gaining ~2 years of in-industry experience as a generalist and art lead on client-funded projects. I'm used to jumping between departments and stepping in wherever I'm needed.

Animation is what I love most — bringing characters to life. I'm looking for roles where I can grow into a more specialized animation position, ideally in a collaborative environment.
