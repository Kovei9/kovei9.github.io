Million Changes

We were approached to create an app prototype designed to help users make small, manageable changes in their lifestyle that help shrink their carbon footprint over time. The client's moto is "It took a million small changes to cause climate change, it will take a million small changes to stop it."

I was brought on to be trained as the Art Lead during the beginning stages of this project. I conceptualized badges that the users would be awareded as they progressed through the game-like mobile app.

Concept Artist Art Lead

Overview

Environment and World Concepts

Quick value studies, color passes, and ideation for spaces these characters could inhabit. Focused on mood, lighting, and clear points of interest rather than final illustration polish.

From Concept to 3D

One of these concept sets was taken further into a full 3D model for a game prototype. The design was tightened, broken down into workable forms, and then translated into clean topology and textures while keeping the original attitude.

Original concept sheet
Final 3D interpretation

The 3D version keeps the main shapes and proportions from the concept but simplifies detail where needed for animation and in game readability. Posing and lighting were used to echo the mood of the original drawing.
