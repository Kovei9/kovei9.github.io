Fish and Game

Fish and Game is an immersive Educational VR Hunting Safety experience, developed by GIMM Works, funded and published by 15+ States within the USA. The goal of the interactive experience is to aid in the Fish and Game Hunting Safety Education Curriculum by providing a safer learning environment that is also easily accessible.

Role: 3D / 2D Generalist Later: Art Lead 3D Models & Props 2D Posters & UI Adjacent Art

Overview

I was hired for Fish and Game as a 3D/2D Generalist, later promoted to Art Lead towards the end of the project.

My Contributions

My work on Fish and Game was spread across both 3D and 2D tasks. Here are some of the key contributions I made to the project:

  • Modeled, UV Unwrapped, Textured, Rigged, and Animated multiple models/assets.
  • Designed 2D Elements, such as lethal shot posters and other supporting graphics.
  • 2D Art for the environmental elements (Mountains, trees, etc)
  • Concept art for the Gun Safety Icon and other UI.
  • 2D Animations for UI icons and other interactive elements.

Work Environment + PipeLine

The hiring process included a test in which I had 2 weeks to model, UV-Unwrap, Texture, Rig, and Animate a low-poly White Tail Deer in Blender. I was in the earlier stages of my 3D courses at the time. However, this studio knew that I was a quick and adaptive artist, always determined to expand my skillset.

I learned on the job, heavily relying on my coworkers for guidance and feedback. Feedback was common and the only reason I was able to grow so quickly in my 3D skills. 3D models were often passed between artists quite often, but none of us necessarily specialized in one area. We were all generalists that could pick up and work on any task as needed. This allowed me to work on quite a few models, and each one taught me something new. Everything I achieved here is entirely thanks to my team and the previous Art Lead. I did the White Tail Deer entirely on my own, asside from some animations. I ended up working on 2D Lethal Shot Posters, UI concept art, and animation icons to indicate player actions. As we approached the end of the project, I primarily aided in decisions related to marketing and client delivery.
